Objetivo

Constituir um acervo técnico para contribuir com a difusão do conhecimento entre colaboradores do CIBiogás e CIH, otimizando o uso das tecnologias de informação e transmissão de conhecimentos.


What is Material Design?

Material Design is a specification for a unified system of visual, motion, and interaction design that adapts across different devices and different screen sizes.


What is the current project status?

Angular Material recently released Version 1 which we consider to be stable and ready for production use. Efforts are currently being focused on fixing bugs and other minor improvements.
